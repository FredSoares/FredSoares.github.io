About Me

I’m Frederico, a graduate in computer management in 2015 and a mobile and self-taught web developer, Cape Verde.

After my university degree in "Computer Management", I got a job as a "developer" freelancer, where I stayed for almost 1 year, but only developed in C#.

In September 2016 I started working on my university's Software house as a developer, and then I started teaching in the computer science department.

During that time, I took online courses such as The Complete Android Course, JavaScript Essentials 2019 Mini Course, React + Redux: Fundamentals and Two Apps of ABSOLUTE ZERO!

Because we know that the best way to learn is to practice, I also did a lot of personal projects with the knowledge I learned in the courses, you can check them in my portfolio.

I always want to learn more, do more and be more. I also firmly believe that we should never accommodate ourselves.

I am hard working, super curious, passionate, committed and also a fast learner!
